Our Story

Founded in 1954, Al Mutlaq Furniture began as a small family workshop dedicated to crafting bespoke furniture pieces. Through decades of innovation and unwavering commitment to quality, we have grown into one of the region's leading furniture manufacturers, serving clients across various sectors while maintaining our founding principles of excellence and craftsmanship.

Our mission is to transform spaces through innovative design and superior craftsmanship, creating environments that inspire, comfort, and endure. We believe in sustainable practices, investing in our people, and maintaining the highest standards of quality in everything we do. Our core values of integrity, innovation, and customer satisfaction guide every project we undertake.
